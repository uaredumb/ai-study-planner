How it works

From chaos to clarity in three steps

No setup, no template wrangling. Give Lumi something to work with and pick what you want back.

1

Drop in your material

Paste notes, summarize an article by URL, or upload a photo or PDF of your textbook. Messy is fine — that's the point.

2

Pick your outputs

Toggle exactly what you need: clean notes, a study plan, flashcards (you choose how many), and a quiz.

3

Study, don't sort

Flip flashcards, run the quiz, follow your plan, and export anything. Sign in to sync it all across your devices.

What's in a study pack

Four ways to learn it — from one input

Every study pack is built around how memory actually works: organize, plan, recall, test.

Clean Notes

Your scattered notes, rewritten into clear, organized bullet points grouped by topic — with the fluff cut and the key ideas bolded. The version you wish you'd taken in class.

Study Plan

A sensible order to tackle everything, so you always know what to do next instead of staring at the pile.

Flashcards

Active-recall cards you can flip in Study Mode. Pick how many you want, then drill until it sticks.

Quiz Questions

An interactive quiz that checks whether you actually get it — with instant feedback and a final score.
